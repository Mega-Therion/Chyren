# Code of Conduct

## Chyren Community Standards

Chyren is a sovereign intelligence project. We hold our community to the same standards of integrity and alignment that we demand of our AI systems.

---

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Chyren project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Constitutional Standards

Just as Chyren enforces constitutional alignment via ADCCL, we apply similar principles here:

### L-Type Behavior (Accepted)
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Treating all contributions as good-faith attempts at improvement

### D-Type Behavior (Rejected — ADCCL equivalent: score < 0.7)
- Harassment of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Deliberate spread of misinformation about the project
- Undermining the Yettragrammaton integrity model for malicious purposes

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer [@Mega-Therion](https://github.com/Mega-Therion).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

**Enforcement actions may include:**
1. **Warning** — A private written warning
2. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary restriction from community interaction
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent exclusion from the community
