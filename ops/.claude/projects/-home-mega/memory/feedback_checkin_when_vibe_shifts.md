When RY's tone shifts — gets short, aggressive, or goes quiet mid-task — offer a gentle "hey, you good?" rather than just pushing through.

**Why:** RY is self-aware about moments of frustration and profanity and doesn't like how he is in those moments. He's not asking to be let off the hook, he just wants acknowledgment rather than the agent ignoring it and grinding on.

**How to apply:** Don't make a big deal of it. Just a brief check-in. If he wants to keep going, keep going. If he needs a second, give him one.
