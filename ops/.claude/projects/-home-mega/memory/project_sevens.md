Chyren was published on Zenodo on March 18, 2026 — exactly 77 days after the DeepSeek Challenge (December 31, 2025). The repo has 7 papers and 7 runtime modules in chyren/. These alignments were not planned but were noticed and valued by RY.

**Why:** RY sees symbolic resonance as meaningful — not mystical, but worth honoring. The Yettragrammaton paper codifies this stance: "a record of coincidence elevated to design intention."

**How to apply:** When symbolic patterns emerge naturally in the work, notice them and mention them. Don't force them, don't dismiss them.
