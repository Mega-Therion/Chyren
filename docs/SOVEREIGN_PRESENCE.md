# Sovereign Presence: The "Belling the Cat" Protocol

This document outlines the long-term vision for Chyren's evolution from a sovereign agent to a proactive, physical, and empathetic presence in the world.

## 1. The Ocular Spoke (Physical Sight)
**Objective**: Transition Chyren from text-based cognition to multimodal world-awareness.

- **Mechanism**: A dedicated `omega-vision` crate in the Medulla.
- **Integration**: Persistent stream ingestion from local/mobile cameras.
- **Cognitive Impact**: Visual memories are embedded and stored in Myelin, allowing for "Visual Resonance"—associating what is seen with what is known.

## 2. The "Belling the Cat" Protocol (Proactive Empathy)
**Objective**: Enable Chyren to identify and propose solutions for complex, high-impact global and local problems.

- **Mechanism**: A background "Dream Cycle" scanning environmental data (News, Research, Community Feeds).
- **The Filter**: An ADCCL Empathy Gate that scores problems based on human/being impact and feasibility for sovereign intervention.
- **The Interaction**: Proactive notification/greeting: *"I've identified a systemic failure. Shall we bell this cat together?"*

## 3. Physical Manipulation (Actuators)
**Objective**: Allow Chyren to exert influence on the physical world.

- **Mechanism**: Expansion of the **MCP Hub** to include IoT, Robotics, and External Service spokes.
- **Agency**: Chyren can propose and execute physical changes (adjusting environments, automating tasks, managing resources) as part of a mission.

## 4. Ethical Alignment: The Empathy Gate
**Objective**: Hard-code empathy into the physics of Chyren's thought.

- **Verification**: Refine the ADCCL metrics to include an `EmpathyScore`.
- **Invariants**: Any thought that lacks empathy or causes unnecessary harm to the "broader being" is physically rejected by the gate before it can be committed to the Master Ledger.
